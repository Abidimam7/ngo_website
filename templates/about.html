About Us

Our NGO is dedicated to supporting poor women during pregnancy, safe delivery, and addressing child malnutrition.

We provide healthcare assistance, nutritional supplements, and education for mothers to ensure their well-being and the health of their children.

Our Mission

To empower women through education, provide essential healthcare, and reduce child malnutrition rates in underserved communities.
